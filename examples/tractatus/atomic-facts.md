# What is the case, the fact, is the existence of atomic facts

What is the case — a fact — is the existence of atomic facts. (2) An atomic
fact is a combination of objects (entities, things). (2.01)

If [the world is the totality of facts](./world-is-facts.md), then facts must
have a structure. If [objects are simple](./objects-are-simple.md), then the
most basic kind of fact is objects combined in a definite way.

In the atomic fact objects hang one in another, like the links of a
chain. (2.03) The way in which objects hang together in the atomic fact is the
structure of the atomic fact. (2.032) The form is the possibility of the
structure. (2.033)

The totality of existent atomic facts is the world. (2.04) Atomic facts are
independent of one another. (2.061) From the existence or non-existence of one
atomic fact we cannot infer the existence or non-existence of another. (2.062)
