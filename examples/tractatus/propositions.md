# The thought is the significant proposition

The applied, thought, propositional sign, is the thought. (3.5) The thought is
the significant proposition. (4)

If [the logical picture of facts is the thought](./thought.md), then the
expression of a thought in perceptible signs is the proposition. The proposition
is a picture of reality. (4.01) It is a model of the reality as we think
it is. (4.01)

Language disguises the thought; so that from the external form of the clothes
one cannot infer the form of the thought they clothe. (4.002) Most propositions
and questions that have been written about philosophical matters are not false
but senseless. (4.003) All philosophy is "Critique of language". (4.0031)

A proposition must communicate a new sense with old words. (4.03) The
proposition only asserts something in so far as it is a picture. (4.03)
Propositions can be true or false only by being pictures of the reality. (4.06)
